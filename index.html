Why we exist

A community, not a conference.

We bring people together around the practices, tools and culture of DevOps — sharing what works, what doesn't, and everything learned along the way.

Continuous improvement

DevOps believes in Kaizen — small, steady steps toward a better way of working.

Networking

Come meet other passionate DevOps practitioners in the community.

Contribution

Every contribution — a talk, a blog post, a question — is welcome.
